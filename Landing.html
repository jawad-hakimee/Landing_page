Infromation About Cyber Security

Cyber security focuses on protecting computer systems from unauthorised access or being otherwise
damaged or made inaccessible. Information security is a broader category that protects all information assets,
whether in hard copy or digital form. Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks.
These cyberattacks are usually aimed at accessing, changing, or destroying sensitive information;
extorting money from users via ransomware; or interrupting normal business processes.
